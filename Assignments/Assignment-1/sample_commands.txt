echo- prints out a line of text
echo hello world prints the phrase hello world.
echo {con,pre}{sent,fer}{s,ed} prints out the first part of the array and
 then the later part of the array. eg: consents consented...prefered.

date- gives you the date and time

whoami- gives the current user id

cal - will give you a specific date in a requested year

In 1752, they wanted to fix the notion of leap year so it would occur
every four years in february. In order to fix this, in 1752, they skipped
a bunch of days in september.

bc- a precise calculator program.
ex echo 5+4 | bc -l  will print out 9
